About This Home

Unveiling YOLO, Sri Lanka's first modern home apartment city, a landmark development redefining contemporary living with forward-thinking design, premier amenities, and superior comfort in Kiribathgoda!

Prime Group, the unparalleled leader in the Sri Lankan real estate industry, delivers an extraordinary residential masterpiece spread over 13 acres of lush landscape in the heart of Kiribathgoda!

This luxurious development features over 476 apartment units, and each apartment block consists of 6–12 units, each boasting a private terrace, offering residents a unique blend of privacy and community living. This architectural marvel stands as a remarkable testament to the power of collaboration and serves as a proud Sri Lankan success story for ages to come. This modern home apartment city is home a to a state-of-the-art clubhouse, introducing extraordinary design features and amenities never before seen in Sri Lanka. Enjoy a variety of leisure and recreational facilities, including;

  • Bowling alley
  • Business lounge
  • Swimming pool with pool bar
  • Karaoke experience
  • Gaming centres
  • Mini supermarkets
  • Coin-operated laundry facilities
