Prime Group’s YOLO Promises a Lifestyle of “Playful Luxury” with Sri Lanka’s First Ever Modern Home Apartment City

  • Located Close Proximity to Kiribathgoda & Kadawatha highway interchange
  • Pay just 1% monthly to secure your spot at YOLO!

Are you ready to escape the ordinary and embrace a lifestyle that screams 'carpe diem' from the rooftops? Ready to trade monotony for a life less ordinary? YOLO is calling, and trust us, you would not want to miss the call. Then buckle up, because YOLO is about to take you on the ride of your life. After all, you only live once - so why not make it extraordinary? At YOLO - life isn't just lived, it's celebrated in bold, vibrant style. It'’'s place where the phrase 'You Only Live Once' is not just a motto, it's a way of life. YOLO is far from being just another apartment complex; it is Sri Lanka's first modern home apartment city, featuring a state-of-the-art entertainment zone that will make you never want to leave!

Imagine escaping the mundane into a vibrant oasis of 13 acres, where 476 exquisite residences await to redefine your lifestyle. YOLO is a pulsating community strategically positioned for the ultimate convenience, placing you just a heartbeat away from urban excitement: just 10km to jet-setting Colombo and even 10 minutes to Kiribathgoda's buzz, a quick 4.5km dash to the Kadawatha Expressway, and a mere 15-minute zoom to catch your flight at Katunayake. YOLO is not only about location - it's about liberation. Here, luxury is not stuffy; it's a playful indulgence that caters to your every whim. From infinity pools to sky lounges, YOLO anticipates desires offering an unrivalled experience in luxury, promising light-hearted extravagance to meet every need.

Immerse yourself in a stunning infinity pool designed to offer breathtaking vistas and sophisticated refreshments from its submerged seating, all enveloped in serene ambiance. Enjoy precision strikes at a premier bowling venue - Sri Lanka’s first bowling alley right where you live! Experience cinema under the stars with an innovative outdoor theatre concept. Collaborate and connect with colleagues in a vibrant coffee workspace. Nightlife thrives at an elevated lounge featuring a cosy fire pit, endless moonlit chit-chats and cityscape views. Lush gardens and courtyards infuse the community with natural vitality. YOLO boasts a Karaoke Bar, Gym and CrossFit centre, single bounce peddle court, coin-operated laundry, and mini mart among countless amenities. 

YOLO revolutionizes home ownership with a redefined interest-free Prime 1% monthly instalment payment plan, making it accessible for first-time buyers and investors while minimizing risk. Investors can expect significant returns, potentially doubling their investment. The project offers flexible exit strategies, balancing affordability with attractive investment opportunities and embodying YOLO's spirit of financial empowerment. Sandamini Perera, Co-Chairperson of Prime Group expressed enthusiasm at the project, stating, “YOLO represents a significant milestone in many aspects. Not only is it a progressive leap for Prime Group, but it’s also a major achievement for the entire Sri Lankan real estate industry. YOLO fuses the best benchmarks of innovation and lifestyle into an unparalleled experience of ‘Playful Luxury’. It’s bold, bright and brilliant. YOLO is an invitation to the young and brave to become a part of a vibrant community cradled in the zenith of real estate excellence. We welcome all those looking for the ideal home to join us at YOLO where hearts stay young for now and always.”

YOLO is the place, to redefine what it means to truly live. Whether you're 30 or 60, if you're young at heart and bold in spirit, YOLO is calling your name.
